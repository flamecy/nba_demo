Go-to-Market Strategy

Two distinct strategies to pitch, based on client readiness.

Scheme A: The "All-in-One"

Native League Pass Integration

Target: NBA League Executives, League Pass Product Heads.

Core Pitch: "Add a new, high-profit interactive revenue module to your existing D2C platform. Seamless experience, maximum ARPU."

Demo Focus:

  • Simulate the League Pass UI perfectly.
  • Show the ALIV layer as a seamless overlay.
  • Highlight in-app virtual good purchases.

Scheme B: The "Second Screen"

Dedicated Team App (Recommended)

Target: Individual Team CMOs, Digital Product Depts.

Core Pitch: "93% of Gen Z is on a second screen. We build your team's 'Digital Arena' App, powered by our Cloud AI that syncs to *any* broadcast (TNT, ESPN), turning lost attention into your team's direct revenue."

Demo Focus:

  • Show a standalone, team-branded app.
  • Show TV in background, app in foreground.
  • Demonstrate **"Cloud Real-time Sync"**: TV audio triggers instant poll on the phone.

Core Tech: The ALIV Cloud

Our advantage is **Cloud-Sync**. All analysis happens on our servers, pushing instant, perfectly-synced interactions to all fans regardless of their broadcast source.

Game Data API

**The "Skeleton"**: Real-time data (clock, score, status) from Sportradar is our primary trigger for all nodes.

Audio Stream

**The "Soul"**: Our Cloud AI ingests the broadcast audio (TNT, ESPN), using NLP to catch commentator emotion and keywords.

Video Stream (CV)

**The "Validation"**: Our Cloud CV engine cross-validates audio/data triggers (e.g., confirms a dunk) and provides a backup sync mechanism.

Audience Chat

**The "Pulse"**: The AI reads the room, analyzing chat velocity and hot topics to trigger relevant polls (e.g., "Bad call by the ref?").
The Golden Windows: Interaction Triggers We strategically target "dead-time" to create engagement without disrupting the core viewing experience.

The Commercialization Loop

How we turn free engagement into direct revenue for the team.

Step 1: Free-to-Play

Fans engage with free content (Polls, Quizzes) to earn non-tradable **"Team Points"**. This builds habit and engagement. Points can be used for prize drawings or free badges.

Step 2: Identity (Revenue 1)

Fans spend **real money** to buy **Virtual Goods** (e.g., "$4.99" official team jersey for their Fan AI Avatar) to show their status and identity.

Step 3: Pay-to-Engage (Revenue 2)

Fans spend **real money** to buy consumable **"Team Tokens"** (like TikTok coins) to fuel their team's "Hype Battle" and get their name on the leaderboard.
